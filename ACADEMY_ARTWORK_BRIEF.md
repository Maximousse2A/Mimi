# Mimi Academy artwork brief

Create 20 landscape hero illustrations. Each image is reused on its lesson card
and at the top of the full article. Until an image exists, Mimi automatically
shows the current abstract SF Symbol artwork.

## Shared direction

- Canvas: 1800 x 1200 px, RGB PNG.
- Keep the cat and important action inside the center 60% for flexible cropping.
- Use Mimi's cream, peach, coral, berry, lavender, mint, and deep-purple palette.
- Keep the tone affectionate, calm, useful, and never clinical or frightening.
- Avoid text, labels, UI screenshots, hard borders, photorealism, and busy scenes.

## Behavior

1. `Academy Behavior Signals` - A vocalizing cat whose ears, eyes, tail, and
   posture are all clearly visible while a human calmly observes.
2. `Academy Tail Language` - One cat shown in a graceful sequence of upright,
   softly curved, tucked, and gently moving tail positions.
3. `Academy Slow Blink` - A relaxed cat and human sharing a quiet slow-blink
   greeting at the same eye level.
4. `Academy Shy Cat Trust` - A shy cat peeking from a cozy safe space while a
   seated human offers a treat from a respectful distance.
5. `Academy Cat Introductions` - Two cats calmly learning about one another
   through a partly opened door and exchanged bedding.

## Nutrition

6. `Academy Calm Mealtime` - A relaxed cat approaching a clean food bowl in a
   peaceful feeding corner with subtle routine cues.
7. `Academy Better Feeding Spot` - A cat eating comfortably from a clean,
   shallow bowl in a quiet location away from busy household activity.
8. `Academy Water Habits` - A curious cat choosing between a fresh water bowl
   and a gentle fountain in two calm locations.
9. `Academy Balanced Treats` - Tiny treats being used for a positive carrier or
   grooming practice, with the main meal still clearly important.
10. `Academy Food Competition` - Two cats eating comfortably at separate,
    visually protected feeding stations without tension.

## Play

11. `Academy Play Patterns` - A cat moving through a playful hunt sequence:
    focused stalk, energetic pounce, and satisfying catch.
12. `Academy Favorite Toy Style` - One cat comparing a ground toy, fluttering
    wand toy, and partially hidden toy with clear interest.
13. `Academy Indoor Hunt Routine` - A cat following a playful route through a
    tunnel, box, corner, and simple food-search moment.
14. `Academy Safe Play` - A supervised cat playing in a clear, non-slip area
    while a human checks and safely stores string-like toys.
15. `Academy Senior Cat Play` - A senior cat enjoying a slow, reachable toy on
    a soft rug with comfortable, low-impact movement.

## Health

16. `Academy Unusual Sounds` - A calm observation moment where a human records
    an unusual sound and notes its context without frightening imagery.
17. `Academy Litter Box Clues` - A clean, accessible litter-box setup with a
    human gently noticing routine changes from a respectful distance.
18. `Academy Early Stress Clues` - A cat showing subtle tension near a quiet
    safe space while household noise and change remain soft background cues.
19. `Academy Calm Vet Visit` - A cat voluntarily exploring an open carrier with
    familiar bedding, treats, and a prepared observation notebook nearby.
20. `Academy Weekly Wellness Check` - A calm weekly check-in during normal
    affection, with subtle cues for coat, movement, food, water, and notes.

## Adding the artwork

Add one image set per required image to `Mimi/Assets.xcassets` using the exact
names above. The Learn tab detects and displays each image automatically.
