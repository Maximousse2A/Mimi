Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Mimi handles information when you use the Mimi iOS app and this support website.

Who We Are

Mimi is operated by SantaLucia. For privacy questions, contact us at maxime.pietri@icloud.com.

Information Mimi Handles

Cat profile and preferences

You can save a cat name, age, avatar choice, interpretation style, listening sensitivity, daily recap preference, and quiz progress. This information is stored on your device.

Audio recordings

When you hold the record button, Mimi records a short audio moment and stores it on your device so you can replay it and keep a history. Mimi does not send audio recordings to our servers for analysis in the current app.

Purchases and subscriptions

Mimi uses Apple In-App Purchase and RevenueCat to offer Mimi+ subscriptions, restore purchases, and confirm active access. Purchase-related information may include subscription status and purchase history.

Analytics

Mimi uses Amplitude analytics to understand app usage and improve the product. Analytics may include product interactions, a device identifier, app version, device or OS information, and approximate location derived from network information. Mimi does not use analytics for third-party advertising.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, iOS lets Mimi send optional reminders. You can change notification permission in iOS Settings.

Support messages

If you contact us, we receive the contact details and message content you choose to provide so we can respond.

How We Use Information

  • To provide Mimi's core features, including profiles, recordings, history, lessons, sounds, quizzes, and subscriptions.
  • To personalize in-app interpretations and educational content.
  • To process purchases, restore subscriptions, and prevent purchase-related errors.
  • To understand product usage, find issues, and improve Mimi.
  • To provide customer support and comply with legal or security obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers needed to operate Mimi, such as Apple, RevenueCat, Amplitude, and hosting providers. These providers process information according to their own terms and privacy commitments.

Tracking and Advertising

Mimi does not show third-party ads, does not use the Identifier for Advertisers for advertising, and does not share data with data brokers. If Mimi's practices change, we will update this policy and App Store privacy information.

Retention

Audio recordings, profile details, and history remain on your device until you delete them where the app allows it or uninstall Mimi. Support emails are kept as long as needed to respond and maintain records. Analytics, purchase, and hosting records are retained according to the relevant service provider settings and legal requirements.

Your Choices

  • Manage microphone and notification permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID subscription settings.
  • Delete Mimi from your device to remove locally stored app data.
  • Contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or other privacy help where applicable.

More details are available on the Privacy Choices page.

Children

Mimi is not directed to children under 13 and is not intended for the App Store Kids category.

International Processing

Service providers may process information in countries other than your own. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as Mimi changes. The latest version will always be posted on this page with the updated date.

Contact

Email maxime.pietri@icloud.com for privacy questions or requests.
