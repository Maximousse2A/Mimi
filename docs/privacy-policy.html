Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Mimi handles information when you use the Mimi iOS app or contact its support team.

Who operates Mimi

Mimi is operated by SantaLucia. For privacy questions or requests, email maxime.pietri@icloud.com.

Information handled by Mimi

Profile, preferences, and local activity

Your cat profile, app preferences, quiz progress, saved interpretations, and ad-frequency counters are stored locally on your device using Apple storage APIs.

Audio recordings

Mimi accesses the microphone only after you grant permission and while you actively record. Recordings and sound interpretations are processed and stored on your device. Mimi does not upload your recordings to a Mimi-operated server.

Purchases and subscriptions

Mimi uses Apple In-App Purchase and RevenueCat to display subscription products, complete purchases, restore purchases, and determine whether Mimi+ access is active. These services may process purchase history, subscription status, app identifiers, and technical information needed to provide those functions.

Analytics

Mimi uses Amplitude to measure product interactions such as page views and button taps. Depending on Amplitude's SDK configuration, analytics may also include a device identifier, IP-derived approximate location, app version, device information, and operating-system information. Audio recordings and cat-profile text are not intentionally included in Mimi's analytics events.

Advertising and consent

The free version of Mimi may display banner and interstitial ads supplied by Google Mobile Ads. Google and its advertising partners may process device identifiers, IP address, approximate location, consent choices, ad interactions, and diagnostic information to deliver, measure, secure, and—where permitted by your choices and applicable law—personalize ads.

Mimi uses Google's User Messaging Platform to request and store advertising consent where required. A privacy-options control is shown inside Mimi when Google requires one, allowing you to review or withdraw applicable consent choices.

Notifications

If you grant permission, iOS allows Mimi to deliver optional notifications. You can change this permission in iOS Settings.

Support communications

If you email support, we receive the address, message, and attachments you choose to send. We use them to respond and maintain appropriate support records.

How information is used

  • Provide profiles, recordings, interpretations, history, lessons, sounds, quizzes, advertising, and subscription access.
  • Process and restore purchases.
  • Understand app usage, diagnose problems, and improve Mimi.
  • Display and measure ads and manage legally required consent choices.
  • Respond to support, privacy, legal, and security requests.

Service providers

Mimi relies on Apple, RevenueCat, Amplitude, Google Mobile Ads, Google User Messaging Platform, GitHub Pages, and email providers. These providers process information under their own terms and privacy policies. Mimi does not sell your audio recordings or cat profile.

Retention and deletion

Recordings, profile information, app preferences, and history remain on your device until deleted through available app controls or by uninstalling Mimi. Support messages are kept only as long as reasonably needed for support, security, and legal records. Third-party providers retain information according to their settings, agreements, and legal obligations.

Because Mimi has no user account or Mimi-operated cloud profile, uninstalling the app removes its locally stored app data. You may contact us to request deletion of support information we control.

Your choices

  • Manage microphone and notification permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Use Mimi's privacy-options control when it is displayed to revise advertising consent.
  • Manage or cancel Mimi+ through your Apple ID subscription settings.
  • Delete local app data by using available deletion controls or uninstalling Mimi.
  • Email us to request access, correction, or deletion of support information we control.

See the Privacy Choices page for practical instructions.

Children

Mimi is not directed to children under 13 and is not offered in the App Store Kids category.

International processing

Service providers may process information in countries other than your own. Their applicable privacy terms describe the safeguards they use for international transfers.

Changes

We may update this policy as Mimi changes. The latest version and its effective date will remain available at this URL.

Contact
