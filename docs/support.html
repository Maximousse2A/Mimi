Mimi Support

For support, bug reports, feature requests, subscription questions, or general feedback, email maxime.pietri@icloud.com.

Helpful Details to Include

  • Your device model and iOS version.
  • The Mimi app version.
  • What you were trying to do and what happened.
  • Whether the issue involves recording, purchases, lessons, sounds, quizzes, or history.

Subscriptions

Mimi+ purchases are handled by Apple. To manage or cancel a subscription, open iOS Settings, tap your Apple ID, then tap Subscriptions.
