Mimi privacy controls

Privacy Choices

These controls help you manage the information Mimi can access.

iOS Permissions

  • Microphone: used only when you hold the record button.
  • Notifications: used only if you grant notification permission.

You can change these permissions in the iOS Settings app at any time.

Local App Data

Mimi stores cat profile details, recordings, history, and quiz progress locally on your device. Delete Mimi from your device to remove locally stored app data.

Subscriptions

Manage or cancel Mimi+ through your Apple ID subscription settings. Apple handles billing and payment methods.

Privacy Requests

For access, deletion, correction, or other privacy requests, email maxime.pietri@icloud.com. We may need enough information to identify and process your request.
