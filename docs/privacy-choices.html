Your controls

Privacy Choices

Mimi gives you several ways to control permissions, advertising consent, subscriptions, and locally stored information.

Microphone and notifications

Open iOS Settings, choose Mimi, and change microphone or notification permission at any time. Without microphone permission, audio recording is unavailable, but other app areas remain usable.

Advertising privacy

Where required, Mimi displays a Google consent message before requesting ads. If a “Privacy choices” row appears in Mimi's profile screen, tap it to review or withdraw the choices managed by Google's consent platform.

You may still receive limited or non-personalized ads where permitted after declining personalized advertising.

Local app data

Mimi stores your cat profile, preferences, recordings, history, and quiz progress on your device. Use available history controls to remove individual content. Uninstalling Mimi removes its locally stored app container.

Subscriptions

Manage or cancel Mimi+ in iOS Settings under your Apple ID and Subscriptions. Use “Restore purchases” inside Mimi to restore eligible purchases.

Support and privacy requests

Mimi does not operate user accounts or a cloud profile database. For access, correction, or deletion requests concerning support messages we control, email maxime.pietri@icloud.com.
