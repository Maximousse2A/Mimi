What Mimi Does

A gentle guide for curious cat humans.

Sound interpretations

Record and replay short vocal moments, then review cautious clues based on intensity, rhythm, timing, and signal quality.

Private sound history

Keep useful sound moments on device so you can replay recordings and compare interpretations over time.

Lessons and quizzes

Short Academy lessons and daily quiz practice help you notice behavior, nutrition, play, and wellness patterns over time.

Mimi is not a veterinary or diagnostic tool. Cats communicate through clusters of signals, and every cat is different. Contact a veterinarian for sudden changes, pain, distress, appetite changes, litter-box concerns, or anything that worries you.
