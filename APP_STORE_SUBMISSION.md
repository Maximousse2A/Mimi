## App Store Metadata - English

### Name

Sound & mood clues

### Promotional Text

New to Mimi? Record a sound, compare body-language clues, and build a calmer routine with short lessons and daily quizzes.

### Keywords

meow,kitten,purr,pet,behavior,body language,quiz,training,care,wellness,routine

Length: 79 bytes, within Apple's 100-byte keyword limit.

### Description

Mimi helps you understand your cat's sounds, body-language clues, and routines with gentle, context-aware interpretations.

Record a meow or short vocal moment, then see a careful reading based on intensity, pattern, timing, and signal quality. Explore common cat sounds, compare body-language clues, and learn what changes are worth noticing.

What you can do with Mimi:
- Record and replay sound moments
- See interpretation confidence and useful context notes
- Build a profile for your cat with age, name, avatar, tone, and listening sensitivity
- Keep a private history of sound moments
- Learn with short Academy guides about behavior, nutrition, play, and wellness
- Practice with daily quizzes and topic challenges
- Unlock Mimi+ for unlimited sound analyses, full history, and every lesson, sound, and quiz

Mimi+ is optional and managed through Apple In-App Purchase. Subscriptions can be managed or cancelled from your Apple ID settings.

Mimi is not a veterinary or diagnostic tool. Cats communicate through clusters of signals, and every cat is different. Use Mimi as a supportive guide, and contact a veterinarian for sudden changes, distress, pain, appetite changes, litter-box concerns, or anything that worries you.
